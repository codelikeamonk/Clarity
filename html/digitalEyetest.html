What is Digital Eye Strain?

The eye fatigue or temporary discomfort in the eyes which is caused after two or more hours of digital device use, is called digital eye strain. Constant use of electronic devices like smartphones, computers, tablets, and gaming systems is the main cause of Digital Eye Strain.

What Are the Symptoms of Digital Eye Strain?

  • Eye fatigue
  • Blurred vision
  • Headaches
  • Red, dry or irritated eyes
  • Back, neck and shoulder pain

What Causes Eye Strain?

One main reason for Digital Eye Strain also known as computer vision syndrome is Blue light, which is emitted by digital devices. It may contribute to vision problems such as cataracts and age-related macular degeneration.
